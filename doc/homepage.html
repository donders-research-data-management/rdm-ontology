Welcome to the Donders Repository

The Donders Institute for Brain, Cognition and Behaviour is a world-leading research centre devoted to understanding the mechanistic underpinnings of human cognition and behavior in health and disease.

The Donders Repository is used by ${userCount} researchers to manage their research data, organized in ${collectionDacDraftCount} work-in-progress and ${collectionDacArchivedCount} finalized data acquisition collections, ${collectionRdcDraftCount} work-in-progress and ${collectionRdcArchivedCount} finalized research documentation collections and ${collectionDscDraftCount} work-in-progress and ${collectionDscPublishedCount} published data sharing collections. Everyone can request access to the published data sharing collections.

  • Publish data to the wider scientific community
  • Safely archive data that cannot be publicly shared to promote scientific integrity and internal reuse
  • Collaborate with colleagues inside and outside of Radboud University
  • Make data findable and citable by assigning a Digital Object Identifier (DOI) and rich metadata to every collection
  • Easily manage who gets access to view and/or edit data
  • Comply with recent research data management movements and current policies of Radboud University to make data more FAIR
